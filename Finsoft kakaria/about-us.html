Our Goal

Kakaria group is a investment platform that empowers investors. We aim to provide affordable, returns. Low-cost Investment is the first step to ending systemic financial exclusion and improving the lives of millions of people.

What We Do

Everyday at Kakaria Froup, we try to develop innovative solutions to provide the lowest-costing credit to our borrowers. We partner with small, local non-profit organizations that work closely with the communities to provide micro-loans and other supportive services. By leveraging technology, we are able to keep our costs low, and pass on these savings to borrowers and social investors. We hope you join us in bringing financial inclusions to the far reaches of India!

People Behind Kakaria Group
